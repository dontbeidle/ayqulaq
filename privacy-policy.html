Introduction

Welcome to Ayqulaq. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting any information related to your use of our website. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

Ayqulaq is a static, content-focused website that collects only minimal technical information. The following data may be collected:

  • Analytics data: Anonymous usage statistics collected through Google Analytics (page views, session duration, general interactions)
  • Technical data: Browser type, device information, operating system, and anonymized IP address. This information cannot be used to personally identify you.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies for analytical purposes. These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the site. You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. All data collected by Google Analytics is subject to Google’s own privacy policies. You can read Google’s Privacy Policy here.

Data Storage

Ayqulaq does not store or manage personal information on its own servers. All analytics-related data is processed and stored by trusted third-party services such as Google Analytics.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data and online activity:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Limit or block analytics tracking (e.g., through browser extensions)
  • Access Ayqulaq stories without providing any personal information

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or data handling practices, you may contact us.
